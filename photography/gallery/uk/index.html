After my trip to Seattle, I traveled back to London, United Kingdom. Quickly, I got addicted to cityscape photography. The birthplace of the Rolling Stones was a fantastic playground within my reach!

Many icons from this city are known worldwide, like Big Ben and the London Eye or St Paul's Cathedral. During my 5 years living there, I explored the monuments in front of the River Thames. Furthermore, I touched upon landscape photography in Hyde Park, without knowing, at the time, that it would become my favorite photography genre.
