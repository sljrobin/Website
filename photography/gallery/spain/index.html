Spain marked a new step in my journey in photography. The first time I visited this beautiful country, I was still focused on cityscape photography. With time, I fell in love with landscape photography.

Consequently, I chose more carefully places where I could be closer to nature. I started with Barcelona, a city with a delightful coast. I continued going back to Spain over the years, looking for places with some history and charm, such as San Juan de Gaztelugatxe. I then felt confident enough to go on a road trip, drive around the Northern Coast, and do my best to capture memorable moments in time.
