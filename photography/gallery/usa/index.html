My journey in photography starts here, in the United States of America. In March 2017, I had the opportunity to travel to this part of the world, and spent one month in the city of Seattle, in Washington state.

As I knew I would not be able to go back there for a while, I decided to buy a mirrorless camera before my departure. Besides the Pike Place Market, the Aquarium, or the ports with all the beautiful sailing boats, I focused my attention on the iconic Space Needle. I could admire it from Kerry Park. I wanted to keep a few memories of that wonderful view.
